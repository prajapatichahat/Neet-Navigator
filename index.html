How It Works

NEET Counselling Process

Follow these simple steps to secure your medical seat through NEET counselling.

1

Registration

Register on the MCC or state counselling website with your NEET score card and documents.

2

Choice Filling

Browse colleges and fill your preferred choices in order of priority during the counselling window.

3

Seat Allotment

Seats are allotted based on your NEET rank, category, and choice order in multiple rounds.

4

Reporting

Report to the allotted college with original documents and complete the admission formalities.
