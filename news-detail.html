April 25, 2026 Counselling

Gujarat state quota counselling schedule released!

The Admission Committee for Professional Undergraduate Medical Educational Courses (ACPUGMEC) has officially released the schedule for the Gujarat State Quota NEET UG Counselling 2026. Students aspiring to secure admissions in medical colleges across Gujarat must take note of the important dates and procedures.

Important Notice: Online PIN purchase and registration will commence shortly. Candidates are advised to keep their original documents ready for physical verification.

Key Dates to Remember

  • Online PIN Purchase: Begins July 1, 2026
  • Online Registration: July 1 to July 15, 2026
  • Document Verification: July 16 to July 20, 2026
  • Choice Filling & Locking: July 21 to July 28, 2026
  • Round 1 Seat Allotment Result: August 1, 2026

Required Documents for Verification

Candidates must carry the following documents in original along with self-attested photocopies to the designated Help Centers:

  • NEET UG 2026 Admit Card and Scorecard
  • Class 10th and 12th Marksheets
  • School Leaving Certificate / Transfer Certificate
  • Caste Certificate (if applicable)
  • Domicile Certificate of Gujarat State

For more detailed information, candidates are requested to regularly visit the official ACPUGMEC portal and stay tuned to our latest updates section. The complete step-by-step process is available on our Counselling Guide page.

April 20, 2026 Admissions

NEET UG 2026 Registration starting soon

The National Testing Agency (NTA) is expected to announce the registration dates for NEET UG 2026 in the coming weeks. Aspirants are advised to prepare their necessary documents, including Aadhar cards, photographs, and signature scans in the prescribed formats.

Registration will only be accepted via the official NTA website. Any changes in the syllabus or examination pattern will be notified through the information bulletin. Students must ensure their email addresses and mobile numbers are active, as all communications from NTA will be sent to these contact details.

April 15, 2026 Updates

New Medical Colleges approved in multiple states

In a major boost to medical education, the National Medical Commission (NMC) has given the green light for the establishment of 12 new medical colleges across Uttar Pradesh, Maharashtra, and Gujarat. This will add approximately 1,200 MBBS seats for the upcoming academic session.

Seat matrix details will be updated on our Seat Matrix page once officially published by the respective state authorities. This increase in seats is expected to slightly lower the cutoff margins for state quota candidates.
