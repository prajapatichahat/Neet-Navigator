MBBS College Categories

Government Medical Colleges

Government medical colleges in the state offer the most affordable medical education with highly experienced faculty and extensive clinical exposure. These institutes are highly competitive and are purely merit-based with very minimal annual tuition fees.

GMERS (Semi-Government) Colleges

GMERS colleges bridge the gap between government and private institutions. Established by the state government but operating on a self-financed model, they offer exceptional quality education at a moderate fee structure.

Private / Trust Colleges

Private and Trust-run medical colleges offer world-class, state-of-the-art infrastructure and facilities. While their fee structures are higher, they provide more seats and are accessible with relatively lower NEET cutoff scores compared to government institutes. Management and NRI quotas are also available.
