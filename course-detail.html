Eligibility Criteria

  • Must have passed 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology
  • Minimum 50% aggregate marks (40% for SC/ST/OBC)
  • Must have qualified NEET UG examination
  • Age: Minimum 17 years at the time of admission
  • Indian Nationality (NRI seats available separately)

Admission Process

1

NEET UG Exam

Appear and qualify the NEET UG entrance examination conducted by NTA.

2

Counselling

Register for MCC/State counselling based on your NEET rank and domicile.

3

Choice Filling

Select your preferred colleges and lock your choices in order of priority.

4

Seat Allotment

Get seat allotment based on rank, category, and available vacancies.
