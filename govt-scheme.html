How to Use This List

Browse through the list of government schemes below. Each card shows eligibility, benefits and a direct link to the official government website where you can apply. Click "Visit Government Website" to go to the official portal for application and detailed guidelines.

MYSY — Mukhyamantri Yuva Swavalamban Yojana

State — Gujarat New

Department of Education, Govt of Gujarat

50% tuition fee assistance up to ₹2 Lakh/year for general category students pursuing professional courses including MBBS in Gujarat. Hostel assistance also available for eligible students.

General Category Up to ₹2 Lakh/Year Annual Renewal
Visit Govt Website

Post-Matric Scholarship for SC Students

Central Govt

Ministry of Social Justice & Empowerment

Full tuition fee reimbursement and maintenance allowance for SC/ST students pursuing post-matric courses (Class XI onwards), including MBBS. Family income limit applies.

SC/ST Students Full Tuition Fee Income Limit: ₹2.5L/Year
Visit Govt Website

Post-Matric Scholarship for OBC Students

Central Govt

Ministry of Social Justice & Empowerment

Financial assistance to OBC students for pursuing higher education including medical courses. Covers tuition fee and provides maintenance allowance based on course type.

OBC Category Tuition + Maintenance Income Limit: ₹3L/Year
Visit Govt Website

Freeship Card Scheme (Gujarat)

State — Gujarat

Tribal Development Dept, Gujarat

Free education for SC/ST students in Gujarat. Tuition fee and exam fees are directly paid to the institute by the state government. Students also receive stipend for books and stationery.

SC/ST Students (Gujarat) Full Fee Waiver Books & Stationery
Visit Govt Website

PM Vidyalaxmi — Education Loan Scheme

Loan & Aid

Ministry of Education, Govt of India

Collateral-free education loans up to ₹10 Lakh for students from economically weaker sections. Interest subsidy provided during moratorium period. Available for medical & professional courses.

EWS Students Up to ₹10 Lakh Interest Subsidy
Visit Govt Website

Central Sector Scheme of Scholarship

Scholarship

Dept of Higher Education, MHRD

Merit-based scholarship for students scoring above 80th percentile in Class XII. ₹20,000/year during graduation. No income ceiling for eligibility but priority to economically weaker.

Merit-Based (80th %ile) ₹20,000/Year Top Performers
Visit Govt Website

AICTE Pragati Scholarship for Girls

Scholarship

All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE)

Scholarship specifically for girl students pursuing technical education. Up to ₹50,000 per year as a tuition fee waiver or academic expenses. Aims to encourage women in professional fields including medicine.

Girl Students Only Up to ₹50,000/Year Income Limit: ₹8L/Year
Visit Govt Website

Post-Matric Scholarship for Minorities

Central Govt

Ministry of Minority Affairs, India

Scholarship for minority community students (Muslim, Christian, Sikh, Buddhist, Jain, Parsi) pursuing higher education. Covers admission fee, tuition fee, and maintenance allowance.

Minority Students Full Tuition Income Limit: ₹2L/Year
Visit Govt Website

Sitaram Jindal Foundation Scholarship

Scholarship

Sitaram Jindal Foundation

Financial support for meritorious and economically weak students pursuing MBBS and other professional courses. Provides monthly stipend and book allowance throughout the course duration.

Merit + Need Based Monthly Stipend Full Course Duration
Visit Govt Website

KVPY — Kishore Vaigyanik Protsahan Yojana

Central Govt

Dept of Science & Technology, Govt of India

National fellowship program for students pursuing basic science courses/research. Monthly fellowship of ₹5,000–₹7,000 and annual contingency grant. Helpful for MBBS students interested in medical research.

Science Students ₹5,000–₹7,000/Month Research Focus
Visit Govt Website

EBC Scholarship — Economically Backward Class (Gujarat)

State — Gujarat

Dept of Social Justice, Gujarat

Scholarship for students from Economically Backward Classes (EBC) in Gujarat with family income below ₹1.5 Lakh/year. Covers tuition fees for medical and professional courses at government colleges.

EBC Students Full Tuition Income Limit: ₹1.5L/Year
Visit Govt Website

National Scholarship Portal (NSP)

Central Govt

Ministry of Electronics & Information Technology

One-stop portal for all central and state government scholarship schemes. Apply for multiple scholarships at once including post-matric, merit-cum-means, and minority scholarships for medical students.

All Students Multiple Schemes Single-Window Apply
Visit Govt Website

Padho Pardesh — Interest Subsidy on Education Loans

Loan & Aid

Ministry of Minority Affairs, India

100% interest subsidy on education loans during the moratorium period for students from minority communities pursuing professional and technical courses including MBBS abroad or in India.

Minority Students 100% Interest Subsidy Income Limit: ₹6L/Year
Visit Govt Website

INSPIRE Scholarship (SHE) — DST

Scholarship

Dept of Science & Technology, Govt of India

Scholarship for Higher Education (SHE) component provides ₹80,000/year for students pursuing natural & basic sciences at the undergraduate level. Applicable for MBBS students with strong science background.

Top 1% in Class XII ₹80,000/Year Merit-Based
Visit Govt Website
