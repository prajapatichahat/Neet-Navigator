About AIIMS New Delhi

AIIMS New Delhi is India's premier medical institution established in 1956 by an Act of Parliament. It is an autonomous institution of national importance and operates under the Ministry of Health and Family Welfare. Consistently ranked #1 in India for medical education, AIIMS offers world-class infrastructure, cutting-edge research facilities, and exceptional clinical training. The institute has produced some of India's finest doctors and medical researchers.
