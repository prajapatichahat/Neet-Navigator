Gujarat Counselling Process

Counselling Steps

Follow these steps for Gujarat NEET UG counselling 2026.

1

Registration

Register on ACPUGMEC portal with NEET score card, personal details, and upload required documents online.

2

Document Verification

Visit designated help center for physical verification of original documents and certificates.

3

Choice Filling

Log in and select your preferred colleges and courses in order of priority. Lock your choices before deadline.

4

Seat Allotment

Seats allotted based on NEET rank, category, and choices. Report to allotted college with original documents.

Important Dates

Counselling Timeline 2026

Key dates for Gujarat NEET UG counselling process.

June 15, 2026

NEET UG Results Declared

NTA releases NEET UG 2026 results and scorecards on official website.

July 1 – July 15, 2026

Round 1 — Online Registration

Register on ACPUGMEC portal. Upload documents and pay registration fee.

July 16 – July 20, 2026

Document Verification

Physical verification of original documents at designated help centers.

July 21 – July 28, 2026

Choice Filling & Locking

Fill college/course preferences. Lock choices before the deadline.

August 1, 2026

Round 1 Seat Allotment

First round allotment results published. Students must accept/reject within 5 days.

August 15 – September 15, 2026

Round 2 & Mop-Up Rounds

Subsequent rounds for vacant seats. New choice filling and allotment rounds.

September 30, 2026

Final Reporting Deadline

Last date to report to allotted college and complete admission formalities.
